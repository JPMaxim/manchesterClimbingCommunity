What is MCC?

Manchester Climbing Community is a website for climbers in and around Manchester. Catch up on the latest news. Meet new climbers and organise climbing sessions. Find upcoming climbing events. Discover new crags and gyms around Manchester. Share your efforts or even get some much needed beta for a project. MCC is the place.

Who is MCC for?

Seasoned professional or complete newbie there is hopefully something here for everyone. Maybe you're looking for a climbing partner, wondering what climbing even is or how to get involved, or just want to try a new spot and want to see what looks good. MCC is for anybody interested in Manchester climbing to whatever extent.
