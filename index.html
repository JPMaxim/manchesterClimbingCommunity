What is MCC?

MCC is a project website made by Jonathan Maxim to showcase skills learnt in HTML, CSS and JavaScript while partaking in MADLAB's Shift Click course. The concept is for it to be a community page for climbers around Manchester to network, find events and read news.

Who is MCC for?

Interested in Climbing news? Looking to find climbing partners and meet new people? MCC is for you! Find upcoming climbing events, discover new crags and gyms around Manchester, share your efforts or even get some much needed beta for a project.
