BLOC-ON RETURNS - WITH LAUNCH PARTY!!! Our BLOC-ON bouldering league returns for the Autumn 2023 edition and we are kicking off round 1 of this years league with a, NOT TO BE MISSED, launch party on Friday the 13th October! Entrance is free from 7pm; and your round 1 points count double for anything you tick on the night! There will be the usual quality blocs to climb along with food & drink and a live DJ. We will be open till 'late o'clock', so pack your climbing shoes, dancing shoes, grab your mates and lets go!
